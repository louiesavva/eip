About

Welcome to Everything Is Pointless \\ A Former Blog

Once upon a time, this was a simple blog about the pointlessness of existence. It was started back in 2007 and rapidly turned into a log of my existential journey. I've now decided to retire the blog. Not because I find everything to be full of point, but because I think the two main pieces of writing (the "book") should be the main focus.

All views expressed on this site are personal and no endorsement by any other party is implied.

Author

My name is Dr Louie Savva. I am a hospital chaplain, working from a non-religious/existential perspective. I also have a PhD in Psychology.

Book

What I'm calling a "book" is actually the two most important posts that I wrote on this blog. Together they outline my weltanschauung or worldview.
